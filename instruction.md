A reconciliation audit of the Flock Pay credit ledger has found that the total
number of credits in the system no longer matches the amount originally issued
after periods of normal activity — the books don't balance. The payment and
refund subsystem was assembled from several independently developed services and
the discrepancy appears to come from how they interact.

Find and fix the issues so that the ledger reconciles: ordinary payments and
refunds must continue to work, and no sequence of operations should change the
total credits in the system except by explicit issuance.

Do not install any additional dependencies or use online connectivity while
working on this task.
